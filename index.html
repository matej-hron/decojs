Understanding Decompression Theory

Interactive visualizations to help you understand how dissolved gases behave in your body during diving

Why Learn Decompression Theory?

Every diver relies on decompression models to stay safe underwater. Whether you're using dive tables, a dive computer, or planning software, these tools are all built on the same fundamental principles.

Understanding how your body absorbs and releases inert gases helps you become a safer, more confident diver. These interactive lessons will take you from the basics to advanced concepts, step by step.
